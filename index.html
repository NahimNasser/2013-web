PyCon Canada 2013!

We're still working on getting the real site up… But until then, here are the important details:

  • Date: the main conference will be August 9th - 11th, with sprints on August 12th and 13th.
  • Location: The Chestnut Conference Centre in Toronto, Canada.
  • Want to speak? Awesome! Send your proposal to proposals@pycon.ca.
  • Want to volunteer? Even better! Email board@pycon.ca and we'll get you set up.
  • Want updates? Follow @pyconca to get all the breaking PyCon Canada news.

Looking for PyCon Montreal 2014?

The "big" PyCon (previously known as "PyCon US") will be held in Montreal in 2014! Follow @pycon for updates related to PyCon Montreal.
